# Royaviators

**Helping organizations turn complexity into measurable impact.**

Royaviators is an impact consultancy helping aviation, tourism, climate, ocean, NGO, university, government, and mission-driven organizations create measurable economic, social, and environmental impact through AI, automation, data, and innovation.

We are not a traditional consultancy and we are not a software company.

We partner with organizations to design, implement, and continuously improve intelligent operating systems that combine strategy, AI, automation, data, knowledge, and workflows.

## Core Positioning

Royaviators helps organizations create measurable impact through:

- AI Strategy
- AI Transformation
- Business Process Optimization
- Automation
- AI Agents
- AI Workspaces
- Knowledge Systems
- Digital Transformation
- Data & Analytics
- Innovation Consulting

## Flagship Platform

### ImpactOS

ImpactOS is the reusable AI Workspace developed by Royaviators. It can be tailored to different industries while sharing one common foundation.

Core modules:

- AI Assistant
- Knowledge Hub
- CRM
- Outreach
- Document Management
- Dashboards
- Analytics
- Automation
- Connectors
- Reporting
- Collaboration
