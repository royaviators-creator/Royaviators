# ImpactOS Architecture

## Product Position

ImpactOS is the reusable AI Workspace developed by Royaviators.

It is not sold as generic software. It is used as the transformation foundation behind Royaviators consulting projects and can be tailored to different organizations and industries.

## Core Idea

**One intelligent foundation, many tailored industry solutions.**

ImpactOS helps organizations connect knowledge, workflows, people, data, automation, and decisions in one AI-powered workspace.

## Core Modules

### 1. AI Assistant

A context-aware assistant trained on the organization’s documents, workflows, goals, and operating context.

Capabilities:

- Answer internal questions
- Support decision-making
- Draft documents
- Summarize information
- Guide workflows
- Assist teams with daily operations

### 2. Knowledge Hub

A structured knowledge base for internal and external materials.

Capabilities:

- Document library
- Searchable knowledge
- Team manuals
- Project intelligence
- Industry resources
- Reusable templates

### 3. CRM

A relationship management layer for partners, clients, stakeholders, donors, businesses, and communities.

Capabilities:

- Contact records
- Segmentation
- Interaction history
- Relationship mapping
- Outreach preparation
- Follow-up tracking

### 4. Outreach

A communication and engagement workspace.

Capabilities:

- Email drafting
- Message personalization
- Campaign planning
- Stakeholder mapping
- Partnership outreach
- Follow-up automation

### 5. Document Management

A central document and asset system.

Capabilities:

- Proposals
- Reports
- Contracts
- Presentations
- Research materials
- Media assets
- Grant documents

### 6. Dashboards

Visual dashboards for operations, impact, performance, and decision-making.

Capabilities:

- KPI dashboards
- Operational dashboards
- Sustainability dashboards
- Outreach dashboards
- Project dashboards
- Impact dashboards

### 7. Analytics

Data intelligence layer for insights and forecasting.

Capabilities:

- Data analysis
- Trends
- Performance insights
- Forecasting
- Segmentation
- Decision support

### 8. Automation

Workflow automation for repeated organizational tasks.

Capabilities:

- Task routing
- Notifications
- Report generation
- Follow-up reminders
- Data updates
- Internal workflow triggers

### 9. Connectors

Integration layer connecting existing organizational tools.

Possible connectors:

- Gmail
- Google Drive
- Google Calendar
- Slack

- HubSpot
- Salesforce
- GitHub

### 10. Reporting

Structured reporting for internal management, funders, clients, regulators, and partners.

Capabilities:

- Impact reports
- Progress reports

- Grant reports
- Management reports

### 11. Collaboration

Team workspace for projects, tasks, knowledge sharing, and decisions.

Capabilities:

- Decision logs

- Comments
- Meeting preparation
